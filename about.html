About SRED Financial

SR&ED, done right —
and priced fairly.

We're a Canadian boutique tax-credit firm focused on one thing: getting our clients the maximum SR&ED refund the law allows, with documentation that holds up to CRA scrutiny — and fees that are meaningfully lower than what the rest of the industry charges.

Our story

Built because the existing market wasn't serving Canadian innovators well.

For decades, the SR&ED consulting market has been dominated by a small number of firms charging contingency fees in the 15–25% range — sometimes higher. For a refund of $200,000, that can mean $30,000 to $50,000 leaving the company. Money the company earned by doing the R&D, redirected away from the next experiment, the next hire, the next prototype.

SRED Financial Inc. exists because that economics doesn't have to be the default. We run lean, we focus on the work that matters most, and we charge less. The result: the company doing the innovation keeps a bigger share of the credit it earned.

What we believe

Three principles. We don't compromise on any of them.

Defensible always beats aggressive.

A claim that maximizes credits but invites a clawback isn't a win — it's a future problem. Every narrative we draft is mapped to CRA's stated criteria. If a reviewer asks a question, the answer is already in the document.

The value should sit with the innovator.

You did the R&D. You took the technical risk. The credit exists to fuel more of that work. Our pricing is set deliberately below industry standard so more of the recovered money stays where it can do the most good.

Technical fluency beats template work.

SR&ED narratives written by people who don't understand the underlying technology are easy to spot — by founders and by CRA. Our team comes from technical backgrounds first, tax backgrounds second.

How we engage

Aligned incentives, on paper, before we start.

No retainers. You pay nothing to begin. We invest our own time on the eligibility assessment and only earn a fee if your claim succeeds.

A single, transparent rate. Our contingency percentage is disclosed in your engagement letter — not buried in fine print. It is set well below the 15–25% range that's typical in the industry.

Audit defense at no extra charge. If CRA selects your file for review, we represent the technical and financial documentation we prepared — without an additional invoice.

No surprises. Every step, every document, every fee is communicated up front. If anything changes mid-engagement, we tell you before it happens.
